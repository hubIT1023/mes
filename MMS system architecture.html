Maintenance Management System (MMS)

Enterprise-grade solution for asset maintenance, checklist management, and work order scheduling

Workflow Overview

1
Create Templates: Define reusable checklist templates with tasks, intervals, and work orders
2
Generate Routine Work Orders: Automatically create scheduled maintenance based on asset intervals
3
Associate Checklists: Link routine work orders to executable checklist instances
4
Execute & Update: Technicians complete tasks and update results in real-time
5
Archive & Analyze: Completed checklists are archived for reporting and compliance
